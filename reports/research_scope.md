# Research scope and claim ledger

## Implemented and executed

- Seven typed evidence-route contracts.
- Real offline sparse, dense, structured, memory, and frozen-snapshot acquisition.
- Sequential budgeted routing with answer/ask/abstain terminals.
- Exact citations, trace replay, conflict display, and privacy failure modes.
- Forced-route counterfactual outcomes, oracle routing, atomic regret, and CPU learned routing.
- Selective-risk calibration, deterministic source shifts, drift detection, and guarantee
  invalidation.
- MiniRoute smoke execution, automated backend/frontend tests, and production frontend build.

## Implemented but not an empirical claim

- Public benchmark adapters for HotpotQA, MuSiQue, and 2WikiMultiHopQA.
- A metadata-only private τ-Knowledge archive validator.
- A live-web route interface, disabled by default.

## Unrun

- Public benchmark comparison to published adaptive-search systems.
- External-model or live-web experiments.
- Human evaluation.
- Large-scale statistical significance analysis.
- Production load, privacy, or penetration testing.

No statement in this repository should be read as a state-of-the-art, institutional-affiliation,
production-readiness, or real-world safety claim.
